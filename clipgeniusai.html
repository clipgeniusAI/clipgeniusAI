Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as one credit?

One credit = one processed YouTube video. From one video, you can generate multiple clips using the same credit.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll maintain access until the end of your billing period.

Do unused credits expire?

Creator plan credits roll over up to 40. Professional plan credits roll over up to 200.

What video formats are supported?

We support all standard YouTube video formats and export to MP4 optimized for social media platforms.
